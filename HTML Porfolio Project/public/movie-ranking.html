My Top 3 Movies of All Time

Interstellar

An epic science fiction film directed by Christopher Nolan, "Interstellar" explores the boundaries of space and time. It’s a visually stunning movie with a compelling story about a team of astronauts who travel through a wormhole in search of a new home for humanity. The emotional depth and scientific concepts make it an unforgettable experience.

The Wolf of Wall Street

Directed by Martin Scorsese, "The Wolf of Wall Street" is a biographical black comedy about the rise and fall of Jordan Belfort. Leonardo DiCaprio delivers a powerhouse performance in this wild and entertaining tale of excess, greed, and corruption on Wall Street. It’s a gripping and often hilarious look at the darker side of the financial world.

The Pursuit of Happyness

Starring Will Smith, "The Pursuit of Happyness" is an inspiring true story about a man’s struggle to achieve his dreams against all odds. The film portrays the journey of Chris Gardner, a homeless salesman who fights to create a better life for himself and his son. It’s a heartwarming and motivational movie that highlights the power of perseverance and hope.
